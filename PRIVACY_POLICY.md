
# Privacy Policy for Linen

**Last Updated:** January 30, 2026

## Introduction

Linen ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address:** Used for account creation and authentication
- **Display Name:** Optional, used for community interactions
- **Avatar/Profile Picture:** Optional, used for personalization

### User-Generated Content
- **Check-In Conversations:** Your reflections and conversations with the AI companion
- **Daily Gift Reflections:** Your written reflections on daily scripture
- **Somatic Practice Notes:** Your notes and experiences with embodied practices
- **Community Posts:** Content you choose to share with the community
- **Prayer Requests:** Prayers you share or request from the community

### Usage Information
- **Check-In Streaks:** Days you've engaged with check-ins
- **Reflection Streaks:** Days you've completed daily reflections
- **Practice Completion:** Somatic practices you've completed
- **App Usage:** Features you use and how you interact with the app

### Technical Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, app version
- **Log Data:** Error logs and performance data for app improvement

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Core Services:**
   - Enable AI-powered reflective conversations
   - Deliver daily scripture-rooted reflections
   - Facilitate somatic practice guidance
   - Support community sharing and prayer

2. **Personalize Your Experience:**
   - Track your streaks and progress
   - Customize content based on your preferences
   - Remember your presence mode and boundaries

3. **Improve Our Services:**
   - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Develop new features based on user needs

4. **Communicate With You:**
   - Send optional reminder notifications
   - Provide important service updates
   - Respond to your support requests

## AI and Third-Party Services

### OpenAI Integration
- Your check-in conversations are processed by OpenAI's GPT models
- Conversations are sent securely and used only to generate responses
- OpenAI's privacy policy applies: https://openai.com/privacy
- We do not use your conversations to train AI models

### Data Processing
- All AI processing follows our strict privacy guidelines
- Your reflections remain private unless you choose to share them
- We implement safety measures to detect and respond to crisis situations

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT sell your personal information.

### We share information only in these limited circumstances:

1. **Community Sharing (Your Choice):**
   - When you explicitly choose to share reflections with the community
   - You control whether posts are anonymous or attributed to you
   - You can delete your shared posts at any time

2. **Service Providers:**
   - Cloud hosting providers (for secure data storage)
   - AI service providers (OpenAI for conversation processing)
   - Analytics services (for app improvement)

3. **Legal Requirements:**
   - When required by law or legal process
   - To protect rights, safety, or property
   - In emergency situations involving imminent harm

4. **Crisis Support:**
   - If you express thoughts of self-harm, we may provide crisis resources
   - We encourage you to contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) directly

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- **Access Controls:** Strict access limitations to your personal data
- **Secure Authentication:** Better Auth for secure account management
- **Regular Audits:** Ongoing security assessments and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access Your Data:** Request a copy of your personal information
2. **Correct Your Data:** Update inaccurate or incomplete information
3. **Delete Your Data:** Request deletion of your account and data
4. **Export Your Data:** Download your reflections and content
5. **Control Sharing:** Choose what you share with the community
6. **Opt-Out:** Disable notifications and optional features

### How to Exercise Your Rights

- **In-App:** Use Profile settings to manage your data and preferences
- **Delete Account:** Available in Profile > Account Settings
- **Contact Us:** Email support@linenapp.com for data requests

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your data while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Shared Community Posts:** May remain visible (anonymously) after account deletion unless specifically deleted
- **Backups:** Deleted data is removed from backups within 90 days

## Children's Privacy

Linen is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## International Users

Linen is operated in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. By using Linen, you consent to this transfer.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- Right to deletion of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending an email notification (for material changes)

Your continued use of Linen after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## What Linen Is NOT

Linen is not:
- A mental health service or therapy platform
- A medical service or diagnostic tool
- A crisis intervention service
- A substitute for professional mental health care

**If you are in crisis, please contact:**

- **Crisis Text Line:** Text HOME to 741741
