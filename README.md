
# Linen

A gentle space for reflection, prayer, and embodied awareness.

## About Linen

Linen provides a faith-rooted, non-clinical environment for spiritual reflection, daily scripture-based gifts, and supportive community connection. The app offers:

- **Check-In Companion:** AI-powered reflective conversations with gentle, non-directive guidance
- **Daily Gift:** Scripture-rooted reflections and somatic invitations
- **Community:** Share reflections, prayers, and support with others
- **Somatic Practices:** Gentle embodied awareness exercises
- **Weekly Recap:** Reflect on your spiritual journey

## What Linen Is

- A space for spiritual reflection and prayer
- A companion for embodied awareness practices
- A supportive faith community
- A tool for gentle self-discovery

## What Linen Is NOT

- Not a mental health service or therapy platform
- Not a medical service or diagnostic tool
- Not a crisis intervention service
- Not a substitute for professional mental health care

**If you are in crisis:**
- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline:** Call or text 988
- **Crisis Text Line:** Text HOME to 741741
- **Emergency Services:** Call 911

## Privacy & Safety

Your privacy is paramount. Linen:
- Does not sell your personal information
- Keeps your reflections private unless you choose to share
- Uses encryption to protect your data
- Provides clear boundaries and safety information

Read our full [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md)
